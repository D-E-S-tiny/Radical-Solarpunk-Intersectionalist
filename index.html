About

I am an anti-theist, intersectional, decolonial eco-communist building futures rooted in collective liberation without gods, masters, or markets.
I reject religion’s chains — not just because it is untrue, but because it enforces obedience, shame, patriarchy, and colonial control.
My ethics come from solidarity, reason, and empathy — not dogma.
I fight capitalism, colonialism, white supremacy, patriarchy, ableism, and ecocide as interlocking systems to dismantle.
I build from below with mutual aid, community autonomy, worker cooperatives, land back, open-source tech, and radical education.
This revolution is joyful, caring, and uncompromising — anti-state, anti-capitalist, anti-colonial, and anti-theist.
We live without gods — but never without each other.

Platform

Manifesto

We stand at the crossroads of history. The old world of exploitation and destruction is dying. We choose a new world rooted in care, freedom, and collective power.

  • We reject capitalism, colonialism, and patriarchy
  • We demand economic justice through communal ownership and degrowth
  • We dismantle the state and build horizontal, participatory communities
  • We fight all oppression together — Black, Indigenous, queer, trans, disabled, migrant
  • We break free from religion’s chains and build ethics grounded in reason and empathy
  • We defend the earth and center Indigenous land sovereignty
  • We abolish prisons and policing; invest in community care and justice
  • We open borders and stand for freedom of movement
  • We build with technology for liberation, transparency, and open knowledge
  • We demand reparations for slavery, colonialism, and apartheid
